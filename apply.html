Apply as a Chief Agent Officer

This application takes around 20 minutes. We read every response. Be specific — concrete examples of what you've built and shipped carry more weight than anything else.

01 — About You
02 — Technical Depth

Name the frameworks specifically. Describe what you built with each and what environment it ran in. We are looking for production deployment, not experimentation.

What problem did it solve? Walk through the architecture and deployment. What failed and how did you fix it?

03 — Operational Judgment

Not just automated — redesigned. What did the process look like before? What changed structurally? What was the measurable outcome?

04 — Communication & Bias to Action

Give a real example if you can. What's your format? How do you manage scepticism or pushback?

What did you ship, what did version 1 look like, how long did it take, and what did you iterate to in version 2?

05 — Environment & Governance

Be honest. Autonomy level, management style, structure preference. The CAO role is high-autonomy, low-structure — describe how that fits or doesn't fit with how you work.

Walk through your process for a new AI deployment that handles customer data. What are your non-negotiable checkpoints?

What draws you to the CAO function specifically, and why CAO Partners?

Anything that demonstrates what you've built. Public repos, articles, side projects, talks.
