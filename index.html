The Role

What is a Chief Agent Officer?

A Chief Agent Officer (CAO) is a dedicated AI infrastructure architect placed inside your organisation to rebuild it from first principles, AI-first. They sit outside your org chart, report directly to the CEO or Chairman, and replace collapsed roles with intelligent agent systems. One person. 100x the output. CAO Partners is Australia's specialist recruitment firm for hiring Chief Agent Officers.

01

AI-First Architecture

Designs and deploys AI agent systems across every department: operations, sales, finance, support, and beyond.

02

Immediate Impact

Starts delivering results within weeks, not quarters. Rebuilds workflows while the business keeps moving.

03

Off the Org Chart

Reports directly to the CEO or Chairman, not the CTO. This is a strategic infrastructure role, not a tech support function.

04

Ongoing Knowledge Transfer

Trains your team, documents the systems, and ensures your organisation owns what's built, not just the output.
