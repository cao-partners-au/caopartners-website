Maps every manual process across sales, ops, finance, and support. Deploys agents that replace the work, not just assist with it. The output is a different kind of business.

Starts with the highest-leverage problem and ships something that works. Then the next one. The pace is not consulting pace. It is founder pace.

Process

Three steps. Weeks, not months.

01

We scope what you actually need

We sit with your leadership team, map your operations, and identify where AI creates the most leverage. No generic briefs. No wasted time. We leave the first conversation with a clear picture of the role.

02

We match someone who can do it

We search our vetted pool and screen for your specific business. You only meet candidates who are genuinely qualified for your problem, briefed on your business before the first call.

03

They embed and build

Your CAO joins your business, reports directly to you, and gets to work. Six weeks to first measurable impact. Everything they build belongs to you. We stay involved through onboarding.

Vetting

Every candidate has been personally vetted.

No keyword screening. No generic shortlists. Every candidate is put through a structured vetting process before they enter the pool. If they are in there, they can do the job.

01

Application screening

Every applicant screened against minimum technical and operational thresholds before interview.

02

Structured candidate interview

A six-dimension assessment framework designed by a BBus (HRM) graduate, former Director of Operations, current Chief Agent Officer, with technical experience across IBM, Apple, Telstra, and Samsung. Technical depth, action bias, business acumen, communication, ethics, strategic thinking. Every candidate scored before they enter the pool.

03

AI-powered matching

Proprietary matching identifies candidates whose capabilities align with your bottlenecks, business context, and budget.

04

MD review before every shortlist

Every brief reviewed by the MD before it reaches you. No one goes forward unless we would place them ourselves.

05

Post-placement accountability

We stay involved through onboarding. Three-month replacement guarantee included as standard. If something is not right, we do not disappear.
